## 🎨 Badge Style Options

You can change the style of badges by modifying the `style` parameter:

- `flat` - Flat style (default)
- `flat-square` - Flat square style
- `for-the-badge` - Large badge style (recommended for main technologies)
- `plastic` - Plastic style
- `social` - Social style (good for follower counts)

## 💡 Tips

1. **Don't Overdo It**: Too many badges can clutter your profile
2. **Group Logically**: Organize badges by category
3. **Keep Updated**: Remove badges for technologies you no longer use
4. **Test First**: Preview badges before committing
5. **Match Theme**: Ensure badges match your profile's visual theme
